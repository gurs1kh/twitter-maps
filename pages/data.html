What is Twitter anyway?

Twitter is an information network made up of 140-character messages called Tweets. It's a simple to explore the latest news related to subjects that you care most about.

How is it useful?

Twitter contains valuable information; it is like being delivered a newspaper whose headlines you will find interesting - you can discover news as it occurs in real time, learn more about topics that are important to you, and get the inside scoop on the latest gossip whether it may be among your friends or even celebrities

How do you use it?

You can BUILD A VOICE by retweeting, replying, or reacting. You can also mention other people in your tweets and initiate a conversation with someone. You can even get fancy by direct messaging people, and favorting other people's tweets. You can even include images or videos within your tweets. The basic idea is to engage with people and follow as many people as you can in order to gain as many followers as you can.
